# Game
## Emoji Phrase Guessing Game
An interactive Android mobile game where players decode phrases, movies, and restaurants represented by creative emoji sequences. Test your emoji interpretation skills across three themed categories with ten randomized challenges per session.
Features

Three Game Categories: Movies, Restaurants, and General Phrases
Real-time Validation: Answers verified instantly through Firebase Realtime Database
Progress Tracking: High scores saved locally using SharedPreferences for each category
Social Sharing: Share your scores with friends via SMS
Ad Integration: Monetized with Google AdMob banner ads
Randomized Questions: Each gameplay session features 10 unique, randomly selected emoji puzzles


## How to Play

Enter your name on the welcome screen
Select a category (Movies, Restaurants, or General)
View the emoji sequence and type your guess
Receive instant feedback on your answer
Complete 10 questions to see your final score
Share your results and compete for high scores!
